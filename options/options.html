Template Details




Tags must be written inside square brackets. All available tags are documented below.

  • [candidate]: Replaced automatically by the candidate's display name on their XP profile.
  • [calendlyLink]: Replaced by the link provided on the "Interviewer Calendly" field located on the email options modal. It will be displayed as a hyperlink with the text Calendly link.
  • [myCalendly]: Replaced by a the link provided on the "My Calendly link" field on the Options Page. It will be displayed as a hyperlink with the text my Calendly.
  • [interviewer]: Replaced by the name provided on the "Interviewer Name" field located on the email options modal.
  • [referral]: Replaced by the name provided on the "Referral Name" field located on the email options modal.
  • [role]: Replaced by the name provided on the "Role Name" field located on the email options modal.
  • [techs]: Replaced by the content provided on the "Tech List" field located on the email options modal.
  • [faq]: Replaced by the link provided on the "FAQ link" field on the Options Page. It will be displayed as a hyperlink with the text FAQ.
